AllerFence

What is AllerFence?

AllerFence serves as a solution for users seeking restaurant options tailored to their dietary needs. It facilitates the process of finding their favorite dining establishments and allows users to place orders with their allergens in place, ensuring they avoid allergens that may pose health risks.


Why should restaurants choose AllerFence?

Because Allerfence handles users allergies for you, it would be easier to catch a users allergen before you accept/complete your order.
